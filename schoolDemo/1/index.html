Jeevan Marg Sophia Secondary School

The foundation of JMSS

Jeevan Marg Convent School was started by Bishop Patrick D’Souza, He was then the Bishop of Varansi, Gorakhpur Diocese. Father Stany D’Souza the founder of this School wanted to hand over the School to some Sisters. At the request of Bishop Patrick D’Souza, The Congregation of the Mission Sisters of Ajmer could not resist the plea of Bishop, an Ajmerian and a Missionary at heart, to assist him in the work of Education. So the Mission sisters took over the Kindergarten School in Deoria in January 1973.

The Jeevan Marg Sophia Secondary School is conducted by the Catholic church, in the Diocese of Gorakhpur. This School is run by the Mission Sisters of Ajmer, members of a Religious Congregation who have dedicated their lives to God and humanity by voluntary service in the educational field.

  • The School was started in a club on 24th July, 1972 by Fr. Stanny D’Souza.
  • The School caters both boys and girls of all castes and creed whether rich or poor.
  • useful knowledge and accomplishments adapted to student's age and social standing
  • Congenial and happy environment for their students
  • Facilities to develop their individual abilities and talents.

A word from the principal to the parents.

Dear parents you have an important role to play as your child goes through different stages of life. . In the Bible we find a passage that tells us exactly what to do and how to do it. For example cph. 6:4 which states “ Do not provoke your children to wrath, but bring them up in the training and admonition of the Lord.” It means that accompanying the child and keeping the communication gap open. Correct her/him when needed, encourage and appreciate all that he or she does in the school or at home. Never compare your child with others, it will lead to their downfall. We the management and the teaching staff are co- partners in rearing and mentoring the children entrusted to us in the school . Thank you dear parents for reposing your trust in us for the growth of your child.

About Us

THE MISSION SISTERS OF AJMER
Today the institutions run by the Mission Sisters of Ajmer, have spread far and wide, over the country. Through the teaching ministry in these schools particularly in the rural areas, in keeping with the Founder’s aim, the Sisters reach out to the needy, the poor, the destitute, the women and children, A light was kindled way back in 1911; it has kept on burning. May it keep on burning, giving God’s love to little ones, his loving children, through the Mission Sisters of Ajmer.
